Truth in Love

“We love because He first loved us.” — 1 John 4:19

Who Is Jesus?

Jesus is not just a historical figure, He is the Son of God, the Savior, the King, and the only way to eternal life. He came to earth to live a perfect life and die for our sins, He rose again to conquer death, and He is coming back soon, just as He promised.

  • He came to earth to live a perfect life and die for our sins.
  • He rose again to conquer death.
  • He is coming back soon, just as He promised.
“I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through Me.” — John 14:6

What Jesus Accomplished by Dying on the Cross

  • He Took Our Sin and Guilt: Jesus carried the full weight of our sins, past, present, and future on Himself.
  • He Satisfied God’s Justice: He willingly endured the punishment we deserved.
  • He Demonstrated God’s Love: The cross is the greatest proof of God’s love for us.
  • He Defeated Satan and Death: Through His resurrection, He broke the power of sin and death.
  • He Opened the Way to God: His sacrifice restored our relationship with the Father.
  • He Gave Us New Life and Identity: In Christ we become new creations.
  • He Showed Compassion Over Sickness and Brokenness: Jesus healed disease and weakness, revealing His power to heal.
“For this purpose the Son of God was revealed: to destroy the devil’s work.” — 1 John 3:8

Repentance — Turning Back to God

What Is Repentance? Repentance means turning away from sin and turning toward God. It’s a heart change that leads to new life.

Why Repent? Everyone sins, and sin separates us from God. Without repentance, we remain apart from Him. “All have sinned and fall short of God’s glory.” — Romans 3:23

  1. Admit you need God.
  2. Feel sorrow for your sins.
  3. Confess your sins honestly.
  4. Decide to turn from sin and follow God.
  5. Believe Jesus died and rose for you.
  6. Commit to living for Jesus every day.

“Repent and be baptized, every one of you.” — Acts 2:38

Prayer — Speaking with the Father

Prayer is our way to connect with God—honest, personal, and powerful. We pray to bring needs to God, seek forgiveness, find peace and guidance, and praise Him.

  • Pray in all circumstances: James 5:13
  • Prayer brings healing: James 5:14
  • The power of righteous prayer: James 5:16
  • Confess and pray for one another: James 5:16
  • Prayer changes circumstances: James 5:17–18

The God We Serve — The Holy Trinity

We worship one God revealed in three persons, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

  • The Father is Creator and Sustainer.
  • The Son, Jesus Christ, is God incarnate who saves us.
  • The Holy Spirit guides, comforts, and empowers believers.
“Go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit.” — Matthew 28:19

Living in Love

Jesus commands us to love as He loved us, selflessly and completely. Love is the evidence of our faith and how we impact the world. “Let all that you do be done in love.” — 1 Corinthians 16:14; John 13:34

Why We Share the Gospel

We share because we’ve been loved and saved and want others to know hope and freedom in Jesus. “Therefore go and make disciples of all nations.” — Matthew 28:19

Ready for His Return

Jesus promised He is coming back soon. Until then, we live in His love and truth, sharing hope with everyone.
